HOW TO BUY

Create a MetaMask Wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device.

Send BNB to Your Wallet

Transfer ETH to your MetaMask or Trust wallet from exchanges like Binance, Bitmart, Coinbase, Gate, etc.

Connect Your Wallet to Uniswap

Connect your wallet to Uniswap by clicking Connect wallet and selecting MetaMask.

Start Swaping

You can start swapping as soon as you have ETH available. Press Select a Token and enter the token address. The token address will be available by launch.
